What's the game?

Both Sides of the Coin is a visual novel with minigames about awareness and morality.

Challenges

Our team encountered several problems. To get a closer look, click the tabs below.

New Technology

Perhaps the biggest challenge we faced: ALL 3 members had to learn at least one new technology for this project-- those technologies being Unity, GameMaker, Ren’Py, and Photoshop.

New Topic

Four days into development, we had to accommodate a significant change in topic after a reassessment of our (the makers’) initial concept.

Sprites

Drawing the sprites-- although the task does not necessarily require heavy thinking, it is tedious and actually took the most time compared to all of our other challenges.
